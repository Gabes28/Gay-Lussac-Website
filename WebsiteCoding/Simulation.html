Gay-Lussac's Law Simulation

This simulation shows how the pressure of a gas changes with temperature using the formula:
P = P₀ × (T / T₀) with T₀ = 300 K and P₀ = 1 atm.

It also visualizes gas molecules moving in a fixed container. As the temperature increases, the molecules move faster.

Calculated Pressure: 1.00 atm

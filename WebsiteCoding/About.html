Fun Facts About Gay Lussacs

This page will let you know about the facts of Gay Lussacs career.

Balloon Adventure for Science, Law and more!

- He ascended over 7,000 meters "23,000 feet" in a hot air balloon to study the Earth's atmosphere
without oxygen equipment. Thus, He measured temperature, pressure, and humidity at high altitudes.

- He discovered that gases expand at the same rate when heated at constant pressure. This led to Gay-Lussac's Law.

- He became a member at a young age and was later elected to the Chamber of Deputies in France, Which he became politician.
